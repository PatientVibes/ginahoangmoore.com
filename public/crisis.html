November 2025 Benefits Delayed
Due to the federal government shutdown, SNAP benefits scheduled for November 2025 are delayed. Benefits WILL be issued - they are just delayed.

‚úÖ What You Need to Do

  • Keep your EBT card: Your card will still work once benefits are loaded
  • Check your balance: Any existing balance can still be used
  • Visit food pantries: Free food available while you wait for benefits
  • Call 211: Get help finding emergency food assistance

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Will I lose my SNAP benefits?

No. Benefits are delayed, not canceled. You will receive your full benefit amount once the shutdown ends.

Can I still use my EBT card?

Yes. Any money already on your card can be used normally.

When will benefits be loaded?

Benefits will be loaded as soon as the federal government shutdown ends. Check your state's SNAP website for updates.
